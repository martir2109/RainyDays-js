Privacy policy

1. Introduction

  • Welcome to RainyDays! Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit or purchase from our website.


2. Information We Collect

  • We collect the following types of information:
    Personal Information:
    Name
    Email
    Address
    Phone Number
    Shipping and Billing Address Payment Information (processed securely via third-party payment providers)

    Non-Personal Information:
    Browser Type
    Device Type IP Address
    Location Data (if enabled on your device) Website Usage Data (e.g., pages visited, time spent on site)

    Cookies and Tracking Technologies:
    Cookies to enhance user experience and analyze website performance.
    Tracking pixels to measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns.


3. How We Use Your Information
The information collected is used for the following purposes:

  • Order Fulfillment: To process and deliver your orders efficiently.
  • Customer Support: To address inquiries, complaints, and feedback.
  • Marketing: To send promotional emails, offers, and newsletters (with your consent).
  • Improvement: To enhance website performance, user experience, and product offerings.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and prevent fraudulent activities.


4. Sharing Your Information
We do not sell or rent your personal information. However, we may share your data with:

  • Service Providers: For payment processing, shipping, and website analytics (e.g., Stripe, PayPal, or Google Analytics).
  • Legal Authorities: If required to comply with legal obligations.
  • Business Transfers: In case of a merger, acquisition, or sale of RainyDays.


5. Cookies and Tracking

  • What Are Cookies?: Cookies are small files stored on your device to improve website functionality and tailor user experiences.
  • Your Choices: You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect site performance.


6. Your Data Rights
Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal and contractual limitations.
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Opt-Out: Opt-out of marketing communications or certain data processing activities.
  • To exercise your rights, contact us at [support@rainydays.com].


7. Data Security
RainyDays implements robust security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data during transmission (e.g., HTTPS).
  • Secure storage of payment details via PCI-compliant providers.
  • Regular security audits and updates.
